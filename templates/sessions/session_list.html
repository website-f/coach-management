Scheduling Rules

Admin-led planning

Only admins can create or update the schedule. Coaches, parents, and headcount get the live calendar instantly after the plan is published.

Recurring sessions

Choose "Repeat weekly for this month" inside the session form, then tick Friday, Saturday, or any weekday combination to generate the month schedule in one step.

Alerts

Every scheduled session can push an in-system notification and Gmail SMTP email to the assigned coach and linked parent accounts.
