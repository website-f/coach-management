About This System

A complete management platform built for your badminton club.

Whether you are a parent keeping up with your child's training, a coach running sessions, or an admin managing the whole club — everything you need is here, simple and clear.

  • For Parents Register your child, track attendance, read progress report cards, and pay clear package fees — RM100 for 4 sessions or RM160 for 8 sessions — all from one parent account.
  • For Coaches View your assigned training sessions, mark attendance during class, and publish detailed skill progress reports for each student.
  • For Club Admin Control everything from one dashboard — member records, staff accounts, billing, payment verification, and club announcements.

Platform Features

Powerful tools, designed to be simple.

Every feature was built with one goal — anyone, including someone using a computer for the first time, should be able to understand and use this system without instructions.

Online Admission & Enrollment

New families apply through a simple online form. Fill in your child's details, choose a training program and location, and submit. The coaching staff will review and approve your application — usually within one to two days.

For Parents

Automatic Skill Level Assessment

During the admission form, your child answers a short set of questions about their badminton experience. The system automatically places them in the right group — Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced. The admin can also adjust this after an in-person assessment.

Smart Placement

Session Monitoring

See every training session your child is enrolled in. View the full schedule, check upcoming dates and times, track which sessions were attended, arrived late, or missed — updated live after each class.

For Parents

Detailed Progress Report Cards

After each training period, coaches publish a structured report card covering specific skill areas — footwork, smash power, serve accuracy, game sense, and consistency. Each area has a numeric score and written feedback. Like a school report card, but for badminton.

Coach Reports

QR Code Payment System

No complicated bank details to copy. When you have an invoice, scan the QR code shown on screen with your banking app, transfer the exact amount, take a screenshot, and upload it as payment proof. Staff will verify and confirm it — simple and safe.

Easy Payments

Live Attendance Tracking

Coaches and attendance staff mark each student's presence during every session in real time. Parents can instantly see whether their child attended, arrived late, or was absent — along with the overall attendance rate over time.

Real-Time

Invoice & Billing Management

All package fees and one-time charges appear as clear, itemised invoices. Parents can see the mandatory registration fee RM60 with 1 free training jersey, plus the monthly package fee for 4 sessions or 8 sessions, with full payment status.

Finance

Official Club Store

Browse and purchase official club merchandise, equipment, and training gear directly from your portal. Admins manage the store inventory; parents and members can shop from the same account they use every day.

Official Store

Notifications & Announcements

Stay informed about session changes, payment confirmations, new report cards, application status updates, and club-wide announcements — all through the built-in notification centre in your account.

Getting Started

From sign-up to first session in 4 easy steps.

Here is exactly what happens when a new family joins the club — step by step, no confusion, no missing pieces.

1

Apply Online

Fill in the online admission form with your child's details, choose a training program and location, and answer a few quick questions so the system can suggest the right skill group.

2

Staff Reviews & Approves

The club admin reviews your application within one to two working days. Once approved, your parent account and your child's member profile are activated and ready to use.

3

Pay the Enrollment Fee

You will receive an invoice for the one-time enrollment fee. Scan the QR code in your portal, pay using your banking app, upload a screenshot of the receipt, and the admin confirms it — usually the same day.

4

Start Training!

Your child attends their first session. From that point on, you can see attendance, read progress reports, check upcoming sessions, and manage the selected monthly package fee directly from the parent dashboard.
