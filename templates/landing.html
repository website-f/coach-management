About the Platform

A complete management system built for sports clubs of any size.

Whether you're a parent tracking your child's progress, a coach running sessions, or an admin managing the whole club — everything is here, connected and simple.

  • For Parents & Guardians Register your child, track attendance, read progress reports, and manage payments — all from one parent account.
  • For Coaches View your session schedule, mark attendance in real time, and publish structured progress report cards for each student.
  • For Club Admins Control everything from one dashboard — members, staff, billing, payment approvals, and club announcements.

Getting Started

From sign-up to first session in 4 steps.

Here's exactly what happens when a new family joins — no confusion, no missing pieces.

1

Apply Online

Create a parent account and submit your child's details. Choose a program and answer a few quick skill questions.

2

Staff Approves

The admin reviews, assigns a coach, and approves the trial — usually within one to two working days.

3

Pay Enrolment Fee

Scan the QR code in your portal, pay via your banking app, upload a receipt screenshot. Admin confirms it the same day.

4

Start Training

Your child attends their first session. Attendance, reports, and payments are all live in your dashboard from day one.
